Tin Coppa

The Romeo of our menu, the Tin Coppa is made with mounds of sliced Capocollo nestled within bubbling, artisan produced mozzarella.

Topped with peppery, pungent arugula and creamy buratta.

Up in Smoked Gouda

A pie that Pedro would surely enjoy! Smoked in Holland's ancient brick ovens over flaming hickory chip embers, this Gouda is a perfect addition to our scratch-made roasted tomato sauce and brick fired crust.

Try it with locally produced mushrooms and pepperoncini!

Nosh Bridges

A Margherita to Juan

Your tastebuds shell-schocked! Silken extra virgin olive oil is spread over our crispy thin crust, then layered with fresh mozzarella and aromatic plum tomatoes before being fired in our intensely hot brick ovens.

This gives our pizza the perfect char, before it is topped with the tastiest organic basil this side of Italy!
